### FULL COPY

Most Kolkata business owners have heard of the Google Maps "3-pack."

Almost none of them know how to get into it.

Here's a plain-English breakdown:

**What is the 3-pack?**
When someone searches "interior designer in Kolkata" or "CA near me" — three businesses appear in a box at the top of the results, with a map. That's the 3-pack. The businesses in those three spots get the majority of clicks. Everyone below them gets almost nothing.

**How does Google decide who's in it?**
Three signals:

1. **Relevance** — does your Google Business Profile clearly match what the person is searching for? Your categories, your description, your services list all feed this.

2. **Distance** — how close is your business to the person searching? You can't control this. But you can control your service area settings.

3. **Prominence** — how well-known and trusted is your business, according to Google? Reviews, backlinks, website authority, and how often people click on your listing all feed this.

**What actually moves the needle in practice:**
- Complete your GBP profile 100% (most have 40–60% completion)
- Get to 25+ reviews with a rating above 4.3
- Post on your GBP weekly (yes, Google Business has posts — almost no one uses them)
- Get your website indexed and optimised for local keywords
- Build citations on Indian directories

This isn't complex. It's just consistent.
